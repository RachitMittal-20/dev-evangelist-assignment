## Project 1 — ContentIQ

A full-stack RAG platform that compares two YouTube videos side by side —
engagement metrics, transcript-grounded chat, and source-cited answers.
Built with React, Node/Express, LangChain.js, and ChromaDB, using
MiniLM embeddings and Groq's llama-3.1-8b-instant for generation.
Live demo compares two MrBeast videos with a 4.74% vs 2.43% engagement
breakdown.

**Stack:** React, Vite, Tailwind, Node.js, Express, LangChain.js, ChromaDB, Groq API
**Link:** content-iq-lake.vercel.app

## Project 2 — Sentiment Analyser AI
